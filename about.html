About Nick

This is my about page. A little bit about me:

I graduated from the University of Iowa in 2014 with a degree in Health & Human Physiology. Up until this point, I was mainly interested in nutrition/food and the exercise/sport sciences. I spent my summers camp counseling kids on overnight trips around the Midwest for an outdoor adventure camp. These summers were epic so I've included a page to share some of the fun. Unsure of how to utilize this degree, my plan after college became to work my way up in these fields of interest. I spent time in numerous restaurant settings and taking personal training classes. Disillusioned from the lack of upward mobility, I looked for change and happened upon The Creative Circus of Atlanta, GA.
